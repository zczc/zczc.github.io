Brief Biography

I am an software engineer in Baidu, inc. I received my M.Sc degree from Science and Technology of China(USTC) and B.eng. degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2012.

My current research interests are data mining and machine learning, especially text mining and human resource analysis. I have won the Best Student Paper Award on CCML'2013 and Best Paper Award Nomination on ICDM'2014.
