Brief Biography

I am a senior research engineer in Baidu, inc. I received my M.Sc degree from Science and Technology of China(USTC) in 2015 and B.eng. degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2012. My supervisor in USTC is Prof Enhong Chen.

My current research interests are data mining and machine learning, especially text mining and human resource analysis. I have won the Best Student Paper Award on CCML'2013 and Best Paper Award Nomination on ICDM'2014. I also serve as a reviewer for several high-rated journals (e.g., ACM TKDD, IEEE TSMC) and the program committee member of several top conferences (e.g., KDD, SDM).
